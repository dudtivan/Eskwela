How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

No complicated setup. No tutorial walls. Just open, add your classes, and Eskwela handles the rest.

1

Sign Up & Meet Esky

Create an account, pick a display name, and meet Esky — your penguin study companion who'll guide you through the app.

2

Add Your Classes

Input your class schedule — subject, room, days, and times. Eskwela builds your week view automatically. Add as many subjects as you need.

3

Plan, Track & Ace It

Add tasks, set deadlines, chat with Esky AI, connect with classmates, and watch your academic life get dramatically more organized.

Who It's For

Built for every kind of student.

Whether you're cramming before finals or trying to build better habits from day one of the semester — Eskwela was made for you.

College Students

Juggling 5–7 subjects, a thesis, and maybe a part-time job? Eskwela keeps it all in one place so nothing slips through the cracks.

Senior High Schoolers

SHS is no joke — STEM, ABM, HUMSS — whatever your strand, managing your schedule and deadlines just got a whole lot easier.

Irregular Students

Non-linear schedules that change every semester? Eskwela lets you fully customize your class days and times — no rigid templates.

Study Groups

Add friends by their unique code and message within the app. Coordinate study sessions, share notes ideas, and motivate each other.

The Chronically Disorganized

If your current system is "I'll remember it" — Eskwela is the upgrade you need. Smart reminders and color-coded urgency will save you.

Filipino Students

Built in the Philippines, for the Philippines. PH holidays auto-sync, and the app's design reflects the local academic system and vibes.
