Delete Your Account

Submit a deletion request and we'll permanently remove your account and all associated data.

⚠️

This action is permanent and cannot be undone

Once your account is deleted, the following will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your Google account info (name, email, profile photo)
  • Your display name and user code
  • Your friends list and all friend requests
  • All friend-to-friend chat messages
  • All developer support messages

🗂️ What happens to your data

  • Deleted from our servers — Your profile, user code, friends list, and all chat messages stored in Supabase will be permanently deleted.
  • Already local — nothing to delete — Your class schedule, tasks, and Esky AI chat history are stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app removes them.
  • Not stored by us — Esky AI messages (Groq) and schedule import images (Gemini) are never stored on our servers.

🗑️ Request Account Deletion

We'll verify your identity using your registered email and user code, then delete your account within 30 days.

Must match the Google account you used to sign in to Eskwela.
Found in your Eskwela profile screen (tap your avatar → Profile).
✅

Request Received

We've received your deletion request and will process it within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email at the address you provided once your account has been deleted.

⏱️ What happens next

  • 📩
    Request submitted Your request is sent to the developer at ivancreatordeveloper@gmail.com
  • 🔍

  • 🗑️

  • ✅
